# VetConnect-WebApp
VetConnect: A simple digital platform connecting veterinarians with farmers and pet owners for consultations and veterinary services.

# Overview
VetConnect helps bridge the gap between veterinary professionals and animal owners by providing an online platform where users can:
• Locate and book veterinary appointments with veterinarians near them
• Consult veterinarians 
• Buy & sell veterinary products through an e-mart
• Receive automated responses through an AI assistant
• Access certified veterinary researches and articles through the vet-blog
• Get real time disease outbreak notifications 
• Doctors and breeders can also scan drugs to test if they are genuine before administration
The goal of VetConnect is to improve animal healthcare accessibility, especially in rural farming communities.

# Features
# The current version of VetConnect includes:
• Home Page: Overview of the platform and navigation to other pages.
• User Authentication: Separate sign-up options for veterinarians and breeders/clients.
• Login Page: Secure access for registered users
• FAQ Section: Answers to common questions for user support.
• Privacy Policy & Terms and Conditions: Legal and usage guidelines for users.
